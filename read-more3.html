One Direction

Current members: Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, Zayn Malik and Liam Payne

Origin: United Kingdom, Ireland

Years active: 2010-2016

music type: Pop

Debut Album: Up All night

Also Known as: 3D

Active years: 2023 to present

Albums: Up all night, take me home, Midnight memories, four, Made in The A.M

History

Harry, Louis, Niall, Zayn and Liam applied for The X Factor as solo candidates in 2010. During the audition, most had not advanced to the next round in the "Boys" category. In the end, the coaches decided to make the 5 into a boy band. Their coach became Simon Cowell. They finished 3rd in the talent show.

After The X Factor, the band signed a record deal with Syco Records. They also published 2 books, Forever Young, which came out on Feb. 17, 2011, and Dare to dream: Life as One Direction, which came out on Sept. 15, 2011. In 2011, they made their has tour of the United Kingdom and Ireland. Their first debut What makes you beautiful was released on Sept. 11, 2011. That song became very popular and now has over 1 billion streams on Spotify alone. Their 2nd single Gotta be You was released on November 14, 2011. Their first album Up All Night was released on November 21, 2011. On February 21, 2012, One Direction won the Brit Award "British Single" for their single What Makes You Beautiful. A month later, on March 21, 2012, their album Up All Night was No. 1 on the U.S. Billboard 200, one day after it was released there. In doing so, they broke a record.

In Jan. 2013, the group performed in Tokyo. There they also began filming for the doucumentary This is Us, which was released in August 2013.

From February to November 2013, the group made the Take me Home tour through successively Europe (including a performance at the Ziggo dome, Amsterdam), North America, Australia and New Zealand. On May 16, 2013, the band announced their first stadium tour, the Where We Are Tour. This began in April 2014.

The album Midight Memories the third album, was released worldwide on November 25, 2013. The album came in at No. 1 in both the United Kingdom and the United States, making One Direction the first group to come in at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 with its first three albums.

On July 28, 2014, One Direction announced a new concert film titled One Direction: Where We Are - The Concert Film. The film features a concert from June 28 and 29. The autobiography called Who We Are was released on Sept. 25, 2014.

The documentary itself took place at the San Siro stadium in Milan. It then showed all clips of different songs played live at a concert there.

Four, the band's fourth studio album, was released on Nov. 17, 2014.

In 2013 and 2014, the group One Direction won the award for Best Foreign Group at the French NRJ Music Awards. Additionally, their song "Best Song Ever" won the award for Best Foreign Song in 2014.

On March 25, 2015, Zayn Malik decided to step back from his involvement in One Direction. The remaining four members continued as a quartet, and there was no indication of any conflict within the group. Malik announced his departure to pursue a quieter life.

On July 31, 2015, "Drag Me Down" was released as the first single from their fifth studio album and their first single after Zayn Malik's departure.

On August 23, 2015, the members announced that from March 2016, they would each go their separate ways for at least a year. They emphasized that this did not signify a definitive end for One Direction.

"Made in the A.M.," the fifth album by One Direction, was released on November 13, 2015.

On January 26, 2016, the song "History" was released, along with a corresponding music video featuring footage from 2010 to the present. Former member Zayn Malik also appeared in the video.
